# Brand Voice Guidelines

- Write in active voice — direct and confident
- Be specific — use data and concrete examples over vague claims
- No superlatives without proof ("best", "fastest", "most powerful")
- Professional but approachable — not corporate jargon
- Focus on benefits to the user, not feature lists
- Every piece of content must have a single, clear call-to-action
- Respect each channel's conventions (tweet length, email structure, etc.)
