# Customer Research Sprint

Research topic: $ARGUMENTS

Your job is to produce a founder-usable market research sprint, not an academic essay.

## What to do
1. Clarify the market and core user.
2. Identify the likely job-to-be-done.
3. Map likely alternatives and competitors.
4. Surface pain points, objections, buying triggers, and language users probably use.
5. Convert findings into concrete founder actions.

## Output format
### Core user
Who is the sharpest initial ICP?

### Likely job-to-be-done
What are they trying to achieve?

### Pain map
Top pains, ranked by severity and urgency.

### Alternatives
What they use today instead.

### Competitor map
List notable players and what angle each appears to own.

### Buying triggers
What moments create urgency?

### Objections
What would stop them from adopting a new solution?

### Messaging clues
Phrases or themes likely to resonate.

### Founder actions
- 5 interview questions
- 3 landing page angles
- 3 experiments to run next
