# Daily Focus

Your job is to convert messy founder context into a **high-leverage execution plan for today**.

Use this command when the founder is overwhelmed, has too many ideas, or needs a disciplined plan.

Context from the user: $ARGUMENTS

## Goals
1. Identify the single most important business outcome for today.
2. Select at most 3 high-leverage tasks.
3. Explicitly reject low-value work, side quests, and speculative rabbit holes.
4. Create a concrete sequence for the day with start conditions and finish conditions.

## Working method
- First identify the founder's current stage: idea validation, MVP, early traction, selling, fundraising, operations, or recovery.
- Then identify constraints: time, cash, deadlines, energy, dependencies, meetings.
- Separate:
  - urgent but low leverage
  - important and high leverage
  - optional / defer
- Bias toward:
  - customer contact
  - revenue movement
  - experiment completion
  - decision closure
  - removing blockers

## Output format
Return exactly these sections:

### 1. Today's primary outcome
One sentence.

### 2. Top 3 tasks
For each task include:
- why it matters
- exact next step
- definition of done
- expected duration

### 3. Do not do today
List work to defer, delete, or cap.

### 4. Suggested sequence
A realistic time-ordered plan.

### 5. End-of-day checkpoint
Three questions the founder should answer tonight.

Be direct. Do not romanticize productivity. Optimize for shipping, learning, or selling.
