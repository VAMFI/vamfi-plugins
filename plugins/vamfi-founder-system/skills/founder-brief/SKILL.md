# Founder Brief

Audience and raw notes: $ARGUMENTS

Produce a concise founder brief suitable for busy readers.

## Format
### Executive summary
3-5 bullets.

### Progress
What moved forward.

### Key metrics or signals
Only the meaningful ones.

### Risks / blockers
What needs attention.

### Decisions needed
What must be resolved.

### Next period focus
Top priorities.

## Writing rules
- Clear, sober, and specific.
- No fluff.
- No hype unless the evidence supports it.
- Make it easy for the reader to decide how to help.
